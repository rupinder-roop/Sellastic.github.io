Precise Audio

These earphones are a result of the innovative technology used by OPPO and Dynaudio. They ensure that you get to enjoy digitally precise audio after thorough research and fine-tuning. Thanks to the DBEE 3.0 Sound System, you will enjoy an immersive sound experience.

Coaxial Dual-driver Design

These earphones have a coaxial dual-drive design to give you a premium feel and experience. High frequencies are managed by the magnetic balanced membrane in the front. And, low frequencies are handled by a triple-layer composite dynamic driver in the back. You can enjoy natural and high-definition audio output with these earphones, thanks to the dual speakers that are placed on the parallel axes.

Magnetic Balanced Membrane Driver

These earphones ensure that you get to enjoy crystal clear audio every time you use them. Their small construction does not come in the way of their audio performance.

Rich and Clear Sound

These earphones make use of the LHDC wireless transmission technology to ensure that more detail is retained so that you can hear every note in rich and clear detail. The dual-core chip and dual-microphone design deliver optimised noise cancellation performance so that you can enjoy disturbance-free listening.

Transparency Mode

Thanks to the Transparency mode of the earphones, you can stay in tune with your surroundings as you can smoothly switch between work and music. This means that you can participate in a conversation without taking off your earphones.

Ergonomic Design

These earphones have an innovative, ergonomic, and lightweight design (which includes silicone tips of two hardness levels) to ensure that you get the best of comfort and stability when you use them.
